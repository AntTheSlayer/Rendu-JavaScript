1. The Champion :

Category : Fighter / Tank

2. Understand the Champion :

Passive : Heal alot and fast

Q Spell: Hit Hard with your hands

W Spell: Tank dammage then reflect them ( peak dopamine)

E Spell: Grab ennemies on your sides and stun them

Ultimate: Grab an ennemy and do him an Air K.O
